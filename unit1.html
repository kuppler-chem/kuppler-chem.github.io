Unit 1: Introduction to Chemistry, Atomic Theory, and Naming Compounds

Standards (Unit 1): HS-PS1-1, HS-PS1-8

Essential Questions

  1. How do scientists use measurement, models, and classification systems to understand matter?
  2. How does atomic structure explain the behavior of matter, and how do naming systems allow scientists to communicate universally?
