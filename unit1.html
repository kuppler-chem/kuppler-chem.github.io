Unit Overview

Lab safety & measurement, quantitative skills (significant figures, dimensional analysis), matter & classification, conservation of mass, atomic structure & history (Dalton→Bohr→quantum model), isotopes & average atomic mass, periodic trends, ionic & covalent bonding, and introductory nomenclature. Concludes with a real-world project.

Standards for Unit 1: HS-PS1-1 HS-PS1-2 HS-PS1-3 HS-PS1-8

Essential Questions

  1. EQ1: How do models of the atom and periodic patterns help us predict and explain macroscopic chemical behavior?
  2. EQ2: How do measurement, error analysis, and conservation laws constrain and validate chemical explanations?
