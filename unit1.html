Unit Overview

Build foundations in measurement/precision, matter & classification, atomic structure (including nuclear basics), periodic organization and bonding foundations, and develop fluency in systematic naming (ionic, covalent, acids, hydrates). Culminates in a real-world project on why naming and structure matter for safety and communication.

Standards for Unit 1: HS-PS1-1 HS-PS1-2 HS-PS1-3 HS-PS1-8

Essential Questions

  1. EQ1: How do scientists use measurement, models, and classification systems to understand matter?
  2. EQ2: How does atomic structure explain the behavior of matter, and how do naming systems allow scientists to communicate universally?
