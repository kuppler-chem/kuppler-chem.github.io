Unit Overview

This unit introduces laboratory safety and measurement, reviews matter and classification, develops atomic structure (including nuclear basics), explores periodic organization and bonding foundations, and builds fluency with systematic naming (ionic, covalent, acids, hydrates). The unit concludes with a real-world project on why naming and structure matter for safety and communication.

Standards for Unit 1: HS-PS1-1 HS-PS1-8

Essential Questions

  1. How do scientists use measurement, models, and classification systems to understand matter?
  2. How does atomic structure explain the behavior of matter, and how do naming systems allow scientists to communicate universally?

▶ Week 1 (Days 1–5) — Safety, Measurement, and Classification
