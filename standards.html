About the Standards

This course aligns to the Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS). Each performance expectation (PE) below includes a brief, family-friendly description, a link to the official NGSS page, and tags showing which units address it.

HS-PS1-1 • Use the periodic table to predict properties

Students use the periodic table as a model to predict relative properties (like reactivity and electronegativity) based on electron structure.

HS-PS1-2 • Structure & properties of substances

Construct and revise explanations for how the structure of atoms and molecules relates to macroscopic properties and behavior.

HS-PS1-3 • Properties before and after reactions

Plan and conduct investigations that compare substance properties before and after interactions to gather evidence of chemical change.

View NGSS: HS-PS1-3

HS-PS1-4 • Energy changes during reactions (models)

Develop models to illustrate how energy changes during chemical reactions, distinguishing between endothermic and exothermic processes.

View NGSS: HS-PS1-4

HS-PS1-5 • Changing conditions and reaction rates

Apply principles and evidence to explain how changes in temperature or concentration affect the rate of reactions.
