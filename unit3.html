Unit Overview

Track energy changes (q = m·c·ΔT), conduct calorimetry, compare endothermic/exothermic processes with models, and explore factors that affect reaction rate. Develop data-driven reasoning and clear, quantitative communication.

Standards for Unit 3:  HS-PS1-4 HS-PS1-5 HS-PS3-1 HS-PS3-4

Essential Questions

  1. EQ1: How do we measure and model energy changes to make reliable claims about chemical processes?
  2. EQ2: How do conditions (temperature, concentration, surface area, catalysts) influence reaction rates in practical settings?
