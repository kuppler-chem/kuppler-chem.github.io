Unit Overview

Balance equations, classify reactions, and quantify chemical change via mole ratios, limiting reactant, theoretical/percent yield, and solution stoichiometry (molarity, dilutions, titrations). Emphasis on quantitative reasoning and careful lab practice.

Standards for Unit 2: HS-PS1-3 HS-PS1-7 HS-PS1-5 HS-PS1-2

Essential Questions

  1. EQ1: How does a balanced chemical equation constrain and enable quantitative predictions about a reaction?
  2. EQ2: How do experimental choices (concentration, delivery, measurement) affect accuracy, precision, and yield?
